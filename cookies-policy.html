🍪 Cookies Policy

Learn how NexaTools uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience.

📅 Last Updated: July 2026reviewed regularly to stay accurate and current.

✅ Simple Summary: NexaTools.io uses a small set of cookies to keep the site running smoothly, remember your preferences, understand how our tools are used, and support the free advertising model that keeps everything free. You're always in control and can manage or disable cookies from your browser at any time.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website places on your computer, phone, or tablet when you visit. They don't harm your device, and they don't let us see files stored elsewhere on your system. Instead, they hold a small piece of information, like a preference you selected or a session identifier, so the site can recognize your browser on future visits.

Think of a cookie as a small note your browser keeps in its pocket. When you come back to NexaTools.io, your browser shows us that note, and we use it to make your experience smoother for example, remembering that you already dismissed a banner, or that you prefer a certain tool layout.

Similar technologies, such as local storage and pixels used by some of the third-party services described below, work in a comparable way and are covered by this Cookies Policy alongside traditional cookies.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

NexaTools.io uses a limited number of cookie categories, each with a specific purpose. We don't use cookies to build invasive profiles of who you are; we use them to keep the platform functional, fast, and free.

Essential Cookies

These are required for the website to function properly. They support core features like page navigation, security, and loading the correct version of a tool. Without these, parts of the site may not work as intended.

Performance Cookies

These help us understand how pages load and perform, so we can identify slow-loading tools or broken elements and fix them quickly.

Analytics Cookies

These help us understand general usage patterns which tools are popular, which pages people visit, and how they navigate the site so we can make informed improvements over time.

Functional Cookies

These remember your choices and preferences, such as a selected tool setting or a dismissed notice, so you don't have to re-select them every time you visit.

Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense)

These support the advertising that keeps NexaTools.io free for everyone. They may be used by Google to show relevant ads and measure ad performance across the sites you visit.

3. Why We Use Cookies

We rely on cookies for a handful of practical reasons, all aimed at making NexaTools.io more useful and sustainable:

  • Improve website performance by identifying and resolving loading issues across our tools and pages
  • Remember your preferences so returning visits feel familiar rather than starting from scratch
  • Measure traffic to understand which tools and blog articles are genuinely helpful to our audience
  • Improve user experience by using real usage data to guide design and feature decisions
  • Support advertising through Google AdSense, which allows us to keep every tool on NexaTools.io free to use

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on NexaTools.io are set by third-party services we rely on to operate and improve the platform:

  • Google AdSense may set cookies to display relevant advertising and measure ad performance
  • Google Analytics may use cookies to help us understand general site traffic and usage trends
  • Google Fonts supports the typography used across the site and may involve limited requests to Google's font servers
  • Future integrations may be added as NexaTools.io grows; this policy will be updated whenever that happens

Each of these third parties operates under its own privacy and cookie practices, which we encourage you to review directly on their respective websites.

5. Managing Cookies

You're always in control of your cookie preferences. Depending on your browser, you can typically:

  • Accept cookies to enjoy the full, intended experience of the site
  • Reject non-essential cookies while still browsing our tools
  • Delete cookies that have already been stored on your device
  • Disable cookies entirely through your browser's settings menu

Keep in mind that disabling certain cookies, particularly essential ones, may affect how some features or tools on NexaTools.io perform.

6. Browser Settings

Most major browsers let you manage cookies directly from their settings or privacy menu, including:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Opera and other Chromium-based browsers

Check your specific browser's help documentation for step-by-step instructions on viewing, blocking, or deleting cookies, as the exact menu location can vary between versions and devices.

7. Privacy

This Cookies Policy is closely connected to how we handle your information more broadly. For a full explanation of what data we collect, how it's used, and how it's protected, please read our Privacy Policy. Most NexaTools.io tools are designed to process your data locally in your browser whenever possible, which limits how much information ever needs to reach our servers in the first place.
