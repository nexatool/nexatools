Why People Are Looking for Canva Alternatives

Canva's free plan has become very limited over the past two years. Here is what is locked behind the paid plan:

  • Background remover only available on Pro
  • Brand Kit saving your fonts and colors requires Pro

  • Download without watermarksome premium elements add watermarks on free plan

For a Pakistani freelancer who needs a logo or business card, paying for Canva Pro every month just to get a clean download is a bad deal. There are better options.

2. Adobe Express (Free Plan)
Adobe Express offers a solid free plan with hundreds of templates for social media posts, flyers, and posters. The quality is high since it is backed by Adobe. The free plan has some limitations on premium assets, but basic design work is completely free. Best for social media graphics and marketing materials.
3. Picsart (Free Plan)
Picsart is popular in Pakistan especially for mobile editing. The free plan allows photo editing, collage making, and basic graphic design. Good for Instagram content creators and students who need quick edits on their phone without a computer.
4. Pixlr E (Free)
Pixlr E is a powerful browser-based photo editor that works like a simplified Photoshop. It is completely free and requires no download. Best for freelancers who need to edit photos, remove backgrounds, or do more advanced image manipulation without paying for Adobe Photoshop.
5. Gravit Designer (Free)
Gravit Designer is a vector design tool available in the browser. It works similarly to Illustrator and is great for creating logos, icons, and illustrations from scratch. More technical than Canva but very powerful for designers who know what they are doing.
