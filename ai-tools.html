Getting the Most Out of an AI Draft

Treat It as a First Draft, Not a Final Answer

The most effective way to use any AI-generated text, including the resume summary writer, is to treat the output as raw material. Read it critically, cut anything that doesn't sound like you, and add specifics the tool couldn't know, like a particular achievement or project.

Add Specific Details Afterward

General AI drafts are strongest on structure and tone, and weakest on specifics. After generating a summary, go back in and add a real number, a specific tool you used, or a concrete result it will make the final text noticeably stronger.

Keep Your Own Voice

Small wording changes go a long way toward making a draft sound like you wrote it. Swap generic phrases for language you'd actually use out loud, and the result will read far more naturally.
