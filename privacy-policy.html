🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Here's exactly what we do and don't do with your information.

📅 Last Updated: July 2026this policy is reviewed regularly to stay accurate and current.

✅ Simple Summary: NexaTools.io does NOT collect, store, or sell your personal data. All tools run 100% in your browser. Your documents and information never leave your device.

🔍 Why Privacy Matters at NexaTools

We built NexaTools around one simple idea: you shouldn't have to hand over your personal data just to build a resume, invoice, or logo. Documents like these often contain sensitive personal or business information, so we designed our tools to process everything locally in your browser instead of on our servers.

This Privacy Policy explains, in plain language, exactly what information (if any) is involved when you use NexaTools, and what our third-party services such as advertising do on this site.

🛡️ Our Privacy First Commitment

Privacy isn't an afterthought at NexaTools it's one of the core values the platform was built on, alongside being genuinely free and easy to use. In practice, that commitment means a few concrete things:

  • Browser-based processing whenever possible our tools are engineered to run in your browser rather than on our servers, so your resume content, invoice details, logos, and passwords stay on your own device.
  • No unnecessary data collection we don't ask for an account, an email address, or personal details you don't need to give us just to use a tool.
  • Transparent policies this page is written in plain language on purpose, without vague legal phrasing designed to obscure what actually happens to your information.

If a future tool ever requires server-side processing for a specific feature, that will be clearly disclosed on the tool's page before you use it, not buried in fine print.

1. 📋 Information We Collect

NexaTools.io is designed to be a privacy-first platform. We do not collect the following:

  • No account registration or personal information required
  • No resume, invoice, logo, or QR code data is ever sent to our servers
  • No cookies used for tracking or profiling users
  • No IP address logging or user behavior tracking
  • No third-party analytics or tracking scripts embedded in our tools

The only data we may receive is standard server access logs (page visited, timestamp, browser type) which are automatically deleted and never used for profiling.

2. 🌐 How Our Tools Work

All NexaTools.io tools Resume Builder, Invoice Generator, Logo Maker, QR Code Generator, Business Card Designer, Password Generator, and Social Media Scheduler process all data entirely within your browser using JavaScript. This means:

  • Your resume content never leaves your device
  • Invoice details are processed locally and exported directly to PDF on your device
  • Logos and business cards are generated on your device's canvas
  • QR codes are generated client-side and never transmitted
  • Generated passwords are created locally and never logged

3. 💳 Payment Information

Pro plan subscriptions are processed exclusively by Gumroad, a third-party payment processor. NexaTools.io never sees, stores, handles, or has access to your credit card or payment details.

When you purchase a Pro plan, you are subject to Gumroad's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. We strongly encourage you to review their policies at gumroad.com.

4. 🖼️ Third-Party Services

NexaTools.io uses the following third-party services on our website:

  • Google FontsTypography files loaded from Google's CDN. Subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • Google AdSenseWe display ads via Google AdSense to support free access to our tools. Google may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can opt out via Google's Ad Settings.
  • Gumroad Payment processing for Pro subscriptions only.

We do not integrate Facebook Pixel, TikTok Pixel, or any other advertising trackers. For more on how cookies specifically are used, see our Cookies Policy.

5. 🍪 Cookies

NexaTools.io itself does not set tracking or analytics cookies. However, Google AdSense (our advertising partner) may place cookies on your device to serve personalized advertisements based on your browsing behavior.

You can manage or disable these cookies through your browser settings or via Google's Ad Settings. Full details are available in our Cookies Policy.

6. 👶 Children's Privacy

NexaTools.io does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Our platform is intended for use by adults and young adults (13+). If you believe a child has submitted information through our site, please contact us at pro@nexatools.io and we will take prompt action.

7. 🔗 External Links

Our website may contain links to external websites such as Gumroad, Google, and social media platforms. NexaTools.io is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies before providing any personal information.

8. 🛡️ Data Security

Since we do not collect or store your personal data on our servers, the risk of a data breach involving your personal information is minimal. We serve our website over HTTPS (SSL encryption) to ensure secure communication between your browser and our servers.

9. ⚙️ Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is typically no data to access, correct, or delete. However, if you have any concerns about data related to a Pro plan purchase (handled by Gumroad), please contact:

  • NexaTools.io: pro@nexatools.io
  • Gumroad directly via their support for payment-related data

✅ Your Privacy Rights

Even though NexaTools.io collects very little personal data by design, you're always welcome to contact us about:

  • Privacy questions if anything in this policy is unclear, we're happy to explain it in more detail.
  • Data requests if you believe we hold any information about you (for example, tied to a Pro purchase), you can ask what it is and request its removal.
  • Reporting concerns if you notice behavior on the site that seems inconsistent with this policy, please tell us so we can investigate.
  • Policy clarification if you need this policy explained in the context of your own situation or jurisdiction, reach out and we'll do our best to help.

You can reach us anytime through our Contact page or by emailing pro@nexatools.io.

🌍 International Users

NexaTools.io is available to users worldwide, and our tools are used by students, freelancers, and small businesses across many different countries. Because privacy laws vary by region, users accessing NexaTools.io from outside the country where our servers are hosted should be aware that their information (where any is involved, such as a Pro purchase through Gumroad) may be processed in a different jurisdiction than their own.

We aim to apply the privacy practices described in this policy consistently for all users, regardless of location, while also respecting any additional rights that may be available to you under your local laws.

📜 GDPR / CCPA Notice

Some of our users are located in the European Union, the United Kingdom, or the state of California, where regulations such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grant specific privacy rights.

Because NexaTools.io's tools are designed to process data locally in your browser rather than on our servers, there is generally very little personal data for us to hold. If you are covered by GDPR, CCPA, or a similar regional privacy law and have a question about how it applies to your use of NexaTools.io, please contact us at pro@nexatools.io and we will respond to your request in good faith. This section is provided for transparency and does not constitute legal advice.

Frequently Asked Questions ❓

Do you store uploaded files?

No. Files and content you work with in tools like the Resume Builder, Logo Maker, or Invoice Generator are processed directly in your browser and are not uploaded to our servers.

How do browser-based tools work?

Our tools use JavaScript running locally in your browser to generate, edit, and export your content, so your device does the processing instead of a remote server.

Do I need an account?

No. You can use every tool on NexaTools.io without creating an account or providing personal information.

Do you sell my data?

No. We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data, and we do not collect the kind of data that would make this possible in the first place.

Do you use cookies?

NexaTools.io does not set its own tracking or analytics cookies. Our advertising partner, Google AdSense, may place cookies to serve relevant ads. See our Cookies Policy for details.

How do I contact NexaTools?

Email pro@nexatools.io or use our Contact page. We aim to respond within 48 hours for privacy-related inquiries.

How often is this policy updated?

We review this policy periodically and update it whenever our practices, tools, or applicable regulations change. The Last Updated date always reflects the latest revision.

Is my information encrypted?

Our website is served over HTTPS, encrypting the connection between your browser and our servers. Since most tools process data locally, there is typically no personal data transmitted to begin with.

Does this policy apply to users outside the United States?

Yes. NexaTools.io is available worldwide, and this policy applies to all users regardless of location, alongside any additional rights available under local privacy laws.

Can I request a copy or deletion of my data?

Since we do not collect personal data through our tools, there is generally no stored data to access or delete. For specific concerns, such as a Pro purchase handled by Gumroad, contact us at pro@nexatools.io.
