Frequently Asked Questions ❓

Q How long does support take to respond?
We normally reply within 24–48 business hours. Messages sent over the weekend are typically answered on the next business day.
Q Do you provide business support?
Yes. For partnerships, sponsorships, or business collaborations, email business@nexatools.io and our team will follow up with the relevant details.
Q Can I report a bug?
Yes. Email bugs@nexatools.io or use the contact form above and select "Bug Report." Please include the tool name and the steps that led to the issue so we can reproduce and fix it quickly.
Q How can I request a new tool?
Use the contact form and select "Feature Request," or email pro@nexatools.io directly. Many of our current tools exist because users suggested them.
Q Do I need an account to contact NexaTools.io?
No account is needed to contact us or to use any tool on NexaTools.io. Simply fill out the contact form or send an email directly.
Q How do I report a security issue?
If you discover a security vulnerability, please email pro@nexatools.io with full details so we can investigate and address it as quickly as possible.
Q Is NexaTools.io really 100% free?
Yes! All tools are completely free to use no credit card, no signup, no hidden charges. Every feature including AI writing, all templates, and watermark-free exports is free.
Q Is my data safe? Do you store my information?
Absolutely. All tools run entirely in your browser. We never collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or documents to our servers, and information submitted via this contact form is only used to respond to you. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
