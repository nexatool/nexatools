✦ About NexaTools

What is NexaTools.io?

A free, browser-based toolkit for everyday productivity.

NexaTools.io started as a simple idea: professional tools shouldn't require a subscription, a signup form, or a credit card. A student writing their first resume, a freelancer sending an invoice to a client, or a small business owner who needs a logo shouldn't have to pay a monthly fee or hand over their email just to get the job done. That's the gap NexaTools was built to close.

Every tool on this site runs directly in your web browser. When you type your details into the Resume Builder or the Invoice Generator, that information stays on your device we don't collect it, store it, or send it to a server. You can open a tool, use it, download your file, and close the tab, without ever creating an account.

NexaTools is built and maintained by a small team focused on keeping these tools free, fast, and genuinely useful. If something is broken or missing, we'd rather hear about it and fix it than add features nobody asked for. If you run into an issue or have a suggestion, the contact page is the fastest way to reach us.
