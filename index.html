✦ About NexaTools

What is NexaTools.io?

A free productivity platform built for students, freelancers and small businesses.

NexaTools.io is a free, browser-based productivity platform that gives everyone access to professional tools no account, no payment, no software installation required. Whether you are a student building your first CV, a freelancer sending invoices to international clients, or a small business owner creating a brand logo, NexaTools has the right tool for you.

All tools run entirely inside your web browser. Your data never leaves your device — we do not store, collect or share any information you enter. This makes NexaTools one of the most private and secure free tool platforms available online.

NexaTools.io is especially popular among Pakistani freelancers on platforms like Upwork, Fiverr, and Rozee.pk who need professional documents without paying expensive subscription fees. Our tools are also used by students at universities across Pakistan, India, and worldwide who need well-designed resumes and presentation materials.
