Free Online UUID Generator

  
The NexaTools UUID Generator creates random version 4 UUIDs (universally unique identifiers) directly in your browser using the cryptographically secure Web Crypto API. Generate a single ID or hundreds at once for database keys, session tokens, test data or API mocks.

  
Every UUID is generated locally on your device, no identifiers are logged or sent to a server, so you can safely generate IDs for sensitive systems.
