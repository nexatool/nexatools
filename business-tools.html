Why Small Businesses Need These Tools

Looking professional as a freelancer or small business doesn't require a design degree or a monthly software bill. Most of the everyday branding tasks sending a clean invoice, handing someone a business card, putting a logo on a website can be done well with a focused, single-purpose tool instead of an all-in-one suite you'll never fully use.

NexaTools' Business Tools collection covers three of those everyday tasks: the Invoice / Billing Generator for getting paid, the Business Card Maker for in-person networking, and the Logo Maker for a first piece of brand identity. Each one runs entirely in your browser, so nothing you type or design is uploaded anywhere.

What Each Business Tool Does

Invoice / Billing Generator

Add your business details, client information, and line items, then choose from 4 invoice templates. The tool supports unlimited items, so it works whether you're billing for a single project or a long itemized job. Once everything looks right, export the finished invoice straight to PDF.

Business Card Maker

Pick from 7 pre-built themes, then customize your name, title, contact details and font. The live preview updates as you type, so you can see exactly how the finished card will look before exporting it as a PNG image ready to print or share digitally.

Logo Maker

Choose one of 5 logo styles and apply your own color palette to generate a simple, clean logo. It's designed as a starting point for a brand identity useful for a new business, a side project, or anyone who needs a placeholder logo before investing in custom design work.
