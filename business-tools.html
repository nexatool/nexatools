Who Are These Business Tools For?

NexaTools' business tools are built for anyone who needs to look professional without hiring a designer or paying for software. That includes:

  • Freelancers who need to send a polished invoice or put together a business card between client projects.
  • Small business owners and shop owners who want consistent branding a logo, business card, and invoice that all match.
  • Startups and solo founders who need a first logo or invoice template before investing in a full brand identity.
  • Anyone billing clients occasionally who doesn't want to pay for accounting software just to send a few invoices a month.

Every tool here runs directly in your browser, so your client details, business information and designs are never uploaded to a server.

How to Choose the Right Business Tool

Not sure where to start? Here's a quick way to decide:

  • If you need to bill a client, start with the Invoice / Billing Generator pick from 4 templates, add unlimited line items, and export straight to PDF.
  • If you need something to hand out in person, use the Business Card Maker to design a card with one of 7 themes and custom fonts, then export as PNG.
  • If you're just getting your brand identity started, the Logo Maker lets you generate a logo in 5 styles with custom colors before you commit to a full design.
  • Building a full brand kit? Use all three together a logo from the Logo Maker looks best carried through to your business card and invoices for a consistent look.

Still deciding? Browse the full tools directory for other categories, or check our blog guides for branding tips.
