Free Online Base64 Encoder & Decoder

  
The NexaTools Base64 Encoder converts plain text into Base64 format and decodes Base64 strings back into readable text, entirely inside your browser. It's a handy utility for developers working with API payloads, data URIs, authentication headers or embedding small files inline.

  
You can also drag in any file to see its raw Base64 representation, useful for building data: URIs for images or checking how a file looks once encoded.
